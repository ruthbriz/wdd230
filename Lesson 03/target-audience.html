Target Audience

The target audience for this website are people who are on the ages eighteen and above who are relying for the weather. Students and also employed people will find this website helpful for them inorder to be able to know the risks and recent updates of the government in the public. It is also better to inform the public about the risks of any calamity that can strike to the places and also inform them on what they can do to avoid this.


Two Persona

Bob

Bob is an office worker who commutes everyday to his work.


Emma

Emma is a stay-at-home wife. She is happily married and has two kids who are four and one years old.
